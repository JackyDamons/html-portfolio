The Best Movies According to Jacky

My top 3 movies of all-time.

To All The Boys I've Loved Before

This is a teen romcom and such a cute love story.

Knives Out

I love a good murder mystery. And I love that this one is a great mix of mystery and quirkiness.

Avengers: Infinity War

I am a huge Marvel fanatic! I love all things superhero-y. And this one was the ultimate for me.
